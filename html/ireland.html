Halloween In Ireland

History Of Halloween

Halloween orignated in Ireland around 2000 years ago.
It oringanted from the ancient Celtic Festival of Samhain.
The Celtic Festival of Samhain the Gods played tricks on their mortal worshipers and even sacrifices were made.

When is it celeberated

It is celebrated on October 31st.
Novemeber 1st was when they celebrated the end of the harvest

Traditions

    Some of their traditions are:

  • They carve radishes and turnips to frighten Jack's wandering soul. Hence the term Jack O' Latern
  • The tradition originated with the ancient Celtic festival of Samhain. The Celtics believed they could not prevail over the troubles of the season or the activities of the deities
  • People would light bonfires and wear costumes to ward off ghosts.
